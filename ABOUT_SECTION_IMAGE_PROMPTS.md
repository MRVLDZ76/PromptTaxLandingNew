## Technical Specifications

### For All Images:
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1200x900 pixels (4:3 ratio)
- **Format**: JPEG, 80-85% quality
- **File size**: Under 500KB for web optimization
- **Color space**: sRGB
- **Aspect ratio**: 4:3 (800x600, 1200x900, or 1600x1200)

### Photography Style Guidelines:
- **Lighting**: Natural window light or professional studio lighting
- **Depth of field**: f/1.8 to f/2.8 for portraits, f/4 to f/5.6 for wider shots
- **Color grading**: Warm professional tones, slight desaturation for corporate feel
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, leading lines, balanced frame
- **Background**: Slightly blurred (bokeh) to keep focus on subject
- **Mood**: Professional, confident, trustworthy, collaborative

### Post-Processing Checklist:
1. Resize to exact dimensions needed (800x600 or 1200x900)
2. Crop to 4:3 aspect ratio if needed
3. Adjust brightness/contrast for web viewing
4. Slight sharpening for crisp details
5. Color correction for consistent skin tones
6. Compress to under 500KB using TinyPNG or ImageOptim
7. Test on different devices/screens

## Recommended AI Tools

### Best Results Priority:
1. **Leonardo.ai** - "Photo Real" model with "Alchemy" enabled
   - Best for realistic professional photography
   - High quality business portraits
   - Excellent lighting control

2. **Midjourney v6** - Raw style mode
   - Great for natural professional scenes
   - Excellent corporate lifestyle photography
   - Use `--style raw` flag for photo-realistic results

3. **DALL-E 3** - Via ChatGPT Plus
   - Reliable for consistent business imagery
   - Good at following detailed prompts
   - Natural compositions

### Settings for Each Platform:

**Leonardo.ai:**
- Model: PhotoReal V2 or Leonardo Diffusion XL
- Enable Alchemy
- Photo Real Mode: ON
- Negative prompts: "illustration, cartoon, animated, CGI, artificial, plastic, fake, text, watermark, logo, signature"
- Guidance Scale: 7-9
- Number of images: 4 (choose best)

**Midjourney:**
- Add flags: `--ar 4:3 --q 2 --s 50 --style raw`
- Avoid: `--style cute` or `--style expressive`
- Use `--no text, cartoon, illustration, CGI`

**DALL-E 3:**
- Specify: "photo-realistic", "professional photography", "corporate style"
- Request: "no text, no graphics, no illustrations"
- Iterate if first result is too illustrative
